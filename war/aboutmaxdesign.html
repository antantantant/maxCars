About this project:

carMesh is an interaction that helps to create car shapes based on user preference. It is also a platform for testing learning algorithms and understanding crowd preference. The 3D modeling scripts follow the tutorials from Learning WebGL, and is based on the THREE.JS engine. Some front-end scripts and style sheet elements are from Soulwire. I would like to express my gratitude to all these and other heros who made this little app to work.
