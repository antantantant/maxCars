What is Matchbox?

Matchbox is an interactive survey for vehicle exterior designs. We built this to collect answers for the question: What kind of car style will you appreciate the most? The difficult of getting this answer from people not only lies on the lack of means to represent a shape verbally but also the fact that people may not be aware of their preference in the first place.

We create this interaction where people explore the designs and the algorithm learns from their feedback and provides more relevant designs in each iteration.

How it works.

In each iteration the user see 9 different designs. The user is asked to pick any number of designs that he/she better prefers than the rest, by double clicking on a design. Picking none of the designs is OK but at least one of the designs should be unpicked. Once this is done, click the Next button and wait for the algorithm to process the data. Another set of designs will be presented and the user can make their choice again. Click the Finish? Click here. button when a good enough design is found.
